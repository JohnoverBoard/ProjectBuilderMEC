ABOUT THE PROGRAM

It takes a community to work as a collective to generate results. We started out with a solution to fight the Imposter Syndrome and forged this platform for students to unite together.
Be a student eager to find a window of opportunity to get started or a mentor looking for a curious bunch of students to team up with, PROJECT BUILDER MEC promises to cater to all needs, for a better tomorrow.
